One common fallacy used in pseudoscientific advertisements is the appeal to authority. Advertisers may use celebrities, doctors, or other authority figures to lend credibility to their claims, even if those claims lack scientific evidence.

In the past it was theoretical dentists and doctors or famous actors used to appeal to buyer. With social media, not only is it licensed professionals and celebrities but also influencers and sponsored testimonials.

It is important to be wary of these appeals to authority and to always look for scientific evidence to support claims made in advertisements.
