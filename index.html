Healing crystal #AD

Healing crystals are believed to be stones or crystals that possess spiritual and healing properties. Users of healing crystals believe that they can promote physical and emotional healing by balancing and channeling energy in the body.
The ethics of advertising healing crystals online with promises of good fortune, aid for mental health, and aid for sleep can be debated. On one hand, users may be aware what crystals can and can not do while others have full faith in their healing abilities. Others argue that these stones can have a positive impact on one's well-being, and that advertising them can help others learn about their potential benefits. On the other hand, skeptics argue that the use of healing crystals lacks scientific evidence and can give people false hope or prevent them from seeking necessary medical treatment.
